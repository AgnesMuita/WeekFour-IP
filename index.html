We're proud of our unique style

Topped with awesome local ingredients and innovative gourmet flavours, our distinctly ‘Kenyan’ pizzas have been loved by the young, old and peckish for over 20 years.

With a huge and diverse menu there’s something at Holy Rollers for everyone so get your laughing gear round a slice today.

It's not Italian pizza. It's not even a New York pizza. It's Kenyan Pizza - a deep base infused with herbs and generously topped with an array of luxurious ingredients that push it beyond the bounds of your average pizza. We're proud to be counted as a Canterbury icon that's been hosting feasts and throwing parties for stoners and all revellers since 1989. We often have an experimental approach to pizza and yet most of our recipes have been with us since the beginning.
